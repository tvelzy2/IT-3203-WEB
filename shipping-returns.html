Shipping & Returns

At Vinny's Vinyls, we strive to ensure a seamless and satisfactory shopping experience for all our customers. With our commitment to providing high-quality vinyl records and exceptional service, we offer a comprehensive Shipping & Returns policy to guarantee your peace of mind. We provide fast and reliable shipping, carefully packaging each vinyl record to ensure its safe delivery to your doorstep.

In the unlikely event that you encounter any issues with your order, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange process within 30 days of purchase. Our dedicated customer support team is always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have regarding shipping or returns. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we are dedicated to making your vinyl shopping experience with us a positive and memorable one.
